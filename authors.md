## Additional details

    macrtools contains the work of talented R developers listed in the COPYRIGHTS.txt
    file:

      Copyright 2017-Present, usethis authors
      Copyright 2017-Present, rlang authors
      Copyright 2020-Present, Simon Urbanek
      Copyright 2013-2014, Timothy Sutton

    The macrtools package integrates these code routines to establish a way to
    setup the R toolchain directly inside of R without needing to expose users to
    the terminal. The R work was done by James Joseph Balamuta with additional
    contributions by contributors listed in the ChangeLog.
